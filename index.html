    Background

    Created by the late Shane McConkey and Dr. Robb Gaffney, GNAR (Gaffney's Numerical Assessment of Radness) is a response to the ski industry taking itself altogether too seriously. Earn points for riding particularly rad lines or achieving particularly hilarious tasks. Lose points for doing anything uncharictaristically lame. Losers pay for beer.

    This game is meant to be played in good fun. Don't lose your pass, get arrested, hurt anyone else on the mountain, or act like a douchebag.
